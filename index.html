About Us



Kangaroo Vision : Transcontinental Friendship



Kangaroo Vision is the materialization of Friendship and brotherhood sans Bordering. An electronic platform of visual communication of experts, for the immigrant malayalees all over the globe , by the film, print and visual media fraternity. Kangaroo Vision, a cost free online entertainment channel designed to foster transcontinental companionship through entertainment programmes. Countless are the gifted artists, writers, performers among the immigrant malayalees. Hardly they get a platform to prove their mettle except certain Onam, Christmas, and New Year functions in their locality. Kangaroo Vision provides a common platform to get together , to perform and to manifest their excellence through the various specialized multinational cultural programmes designed by the channel thorugh which open a new vista of specialised employment.All the multinational cultural programmes and features are specially designed and designated in this aspect. Any immigrant malayalee from any part of the globe is entitled to use this visual platform without broadcasting fee and those who stands exceptional may get remuneration accordingly.Soliciting the whole hearted cooperation from the immigrant malayalees all over.
